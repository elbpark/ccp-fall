Grilled Burger Patties - Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbons (PAHs)

How is it formed?

When organic materials containing carbon, such as burger patties, are heated directly while being processed (e.g. grilled, pan-seared), PAHs can enter the meat.
PAHs can be carcinogenic (they are able to cause cancer) but the general health risk for someone consuming them in moderation is quite low.
