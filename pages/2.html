Chemical structure of acrylamide

How is it formed?

When bread is heated, the sugars and amino acids inside react during the same reaction that darkens the bread- the Maillard reaction.
This creates acrylamide. Acrylamide is a potential carcinogen (a substance with cancer-causing capabilities) but the average human consumption of it thankfully isn't enough to cause cancer.
